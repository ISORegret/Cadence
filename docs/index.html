Everything you need between paychecks

Cadence isn’t a bank feed — it’s a planning layer. You tell it your pay schedule and what’s coming due; it shows the runway.

Calendar & upcoming

Bills and withdrawals on a timeline so you’re not surprised the day before payday.

Cashflow & balance

Optional starting balance plus scheduled items to estimate where your checking balance lands.

Envelopes & budgets

Category or envelope targets for the current pay window — compare plan vs actual.

Bills & one-offs

Recurring bills with flexible schedules, plus one-time hits so nothing gets lost.

Debt tool

A dedicated space to model payoff plans alongside the rest of your cashflow.

Export & backup

JSON backup, CSV, and PDF summaries — your data is yours to keep.

Up and running in minutes

Check off & adjust
